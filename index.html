Privacy Policy

SafeGen runs entirely in your browser. Passwords are generated locally using your device, and nothing is ever sent to a server — there are no network requests in this app. The only data stored is your display preference (theme, accent, and default length) in your browser's localStorage, so settings persist between visits. No analytics, no tracking, no third-party scripts. To clear stored preferences, use the Settings panel or clear your browser's site data. For privacy questions, reach out via the Email link above. Last updated: June 2026.

Generated passwords are not stored or transmitted by SafeGen — once you close or refresh the page, a generated password is gone unless you've copied or saved it yourself.
